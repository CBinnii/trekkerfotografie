Bio

Dave is a director/photographer based in Los Angeles. He got his start shooting album covers for 50 Cent, Chris Brown, Three 6 Mafia, Soulja Boy, etc. This led to shooting national advertising campaigns.

Dave has been collaborating with the top agencies and brands, directing TVC’s across the globe that combine his love of adventure, people, fashion, and cars. With a background in skating and snowboarding, he strives to tell stories that are fast-paced and energetic, always keeping the camera moving
